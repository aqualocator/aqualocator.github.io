AQUALocator

 

Here at Aqua folks can use the AQUALOCATOR app to help find and locate fellow Aqua employees!
Have questions or need further help? Please contact Chuck Konkol; ext. 4574 or email ckonkol@aqua-aerobic.com

 

Here are some tips on how to use this great resource!

1.       Search employees by tapping [Enter] button

2.       Search employees

o   Last Name

o   First Name

o   Department Name

3.       Connect multiple ways

o   Viewing Employee Photo
Great way to put a name to a face!

o   Creating & Sending Email
Tap on email to create & send email!

o   Viewing Others in Their Department
Tap on department name to view all employees in that department

4.       Locate employees location

o   Searching and finding employee by first name, last name or department name

o   Tap on the Locate button

o   View location!

5.       Are you unable find someone or need to update information about yourself or someone else?

o   Tap Update Profile on home page of app!

6.       Watch Help Video
